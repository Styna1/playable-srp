Downloading and installing Playable-SRP is extremely easy! click below depending on ur launcher.
<details>
<summary>Modrinth Launcher</summary>

To start, go to the `Discover Content` page, from here you will:
* Search for "Playable-SRP" in the modpacks section
* click "install" on our modpack.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Prism Launcher</summary>

First, we are going to click `âž• Add Instance`.

In this screen, choose Modrinth, and search for Playable-SRP. Then choose the latest version of the modpack and click `OK`!

</details>

<details>
<summary>Curseforge Launcher</summary>

To start, click the search bar when on the minecraft section
* Search for "Playable-SRP" in the modpacks section
* click "install" on our modpack (it is the one by styna if we aren't the number one result).

</details>
