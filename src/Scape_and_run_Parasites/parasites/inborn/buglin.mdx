## Spawning

* Spawns naturally, from Phase 0 through Phase 2, with a spawnweight of 30, in groups of 2 to 6.
* Spawned by certain Assimilated Mobs after exploding on death.
* Spawned by Tunnel blocks.
* Spawned by Flying Carriers.
* Spawned by Monarchs.

## Behavior

Buglins are Transition units as well as Infection Propagators.

    * Buglins are unable to attack, fleeing from Aggressive Mobs and Players.
    * Buglins will grow into Rupters after a period of 75 seconds.
    * Buglins will apply Call Of The Hive when touched, duration increases the longer contact is made.

*Weaknesses*

    * Like all Parasites, Buglins are weak to fire. Damage inflicted by fire is quadrupled.
