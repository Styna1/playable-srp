Nexus Parasites are a unique Tier of Parasites. This Tier placement is relatively arbitrary, as they evolve while staying in the same Tier. This Tier contains 3 unique Mobs, each with 4 Stages, with 12 Mobs in total.

Parasites part of the Nexus Tier depending on the stage, must be approached with caution because of their game changing capabilities, but must be killed as soon as possible to prevent further catastrophe.
