Primitive Parasites are a Tier of Parasites. This Tier is placed above Assimilated Mobs and contains 12 Mobs.

Parasites part of the Primitive Tier should be approached with caution because of their strong Stats and unique Abilities. Killing any of them grants the player the "Primitive" advancement, and killing each of the mobs listed here at least once will grant Ruin of Fools.
